Beer30

A Brewery Recommender for Travellers


Beer30 is a tool to help you find the right brewery to go to on your next trip!

Enter a location and either a descriptive keyword or a specific beer that you like,
and Beer30 will find breweries that make beer that are closely related to your search
terms. Beer30 is powered by a deep neural network and is trained on over 2 million
text reviews from BeerAdvocate from 1998-2017.

Instructions
  1. Enter a location:
    • Enter City and State to search US and Canada
    • Leave City blank and enter a State to search an entire state or province
    • To search internationally, enter the City and leave State blank
  2. Choose Descriptive Word or Specific Beer
    • Specific Beer must be spelled & formatted correctly - check BeerAdvocate if you are having trouble
    • Descriptive Word must be a single word. Examples:
      • Beer Styles: IPA - NEIPA - Stout - Sour
      • Hoppy - Hops - Hoptastic - Dank
      • Funky - Brett - Stinky
      • Dark - Heavy - Bright
      • Citrus - Fruity - Bitter
  3. Search!
